About DarKrypt Diary

DarKrypt Diary is a personal encrypted diary app that allows you to securely store your private messages, thoughts, and notes. Each entry is encrypted with the Vigenère cipher, ensuring that your data remains safe and protected.

Features:

  • Create, edit, and view encrypted diary entries.
  • Each note is encrypted with a unique cipher key.
  • Decrypt your notes whenever you need them, keeping your secrets safe.
  • Login and manage your encrypted notes.
